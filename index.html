About the Event

About the Workshop

In alignment with the vision of Viksit Bharat, this workshop empowers educators to develop inclusive, secure, and ethical AI solutions for India's diverse socio-linguistic landscape. It focuses on multilingual AI systems like voice assistants and chatbots, emphasizing cybersecurity, ethical deployment, and accessibility for regional communities.

About AICTE-VAANI

The AICTE-Vibrant Advocacy for Advancement and Nurturing of Indian Languages (VAANI) scheme promotes Indian languages in technical education to improve the teaching-learning process. It aims to create a knowledge base in local languages and encourage research and industry collaboration in regional languages.

Objectives & Expected Outcomes

Key Objectives

  • Equip educators with skills in developing inclusive AI solutions.
  • Design bias-free, secure AI tools for non-English users.
  • Promote responsible AI usage in governance, healthcare, and agriculture.
  • Build AI solutions that serve regional languages and communities.

Expected Outcomes

  • Ability to design secure, multilingual AI systems.
  • Skills to combat AI bias and enhance cybersecurity.
  • Capability for ethical AI implementation across critical sectors.
  • Expertise in creating accessible, region-specific AI tools.
